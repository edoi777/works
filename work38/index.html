Bartini.aero Vision 概念
Bartini technology is designed so that one full lifecycle of one aerial vehicle shall correspond to approximately 700,000 McFly tokens, much like full lifecycle of a modern automobile approximates to 500,000 km of driving. Tokens therefore are used to measure the total life cycle of all components by expending tokens while the latter perform their respective functions. Bartini技术的目的是使一台飞行器完整的生命周期有相应约700000 McFly代币，如同一辆现代汽车的完整生命周期的行驶里程约500000公里。因此，当所有的部件通过消耗代币执行各自的功能后，代币，被用于测量它们的总生命周期。
